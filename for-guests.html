Welcome to the Future of Hotel Stays

At CustomHost, we believe that every guest is unique — and your hotel experience should reflect that. With our smart system, you can personalize everything from room temperature to lighting, choose your preferred pillow type, pre-order meals, and even set up your own access preferences before arriving.

  • Adjust lighting and ambiance to your mood
  • Choose your ideal sleep setup
  • Pre-select meals based on dietary preferences
  • Access your room using biometric authentication
  • Seamless high-speed internet connection

No more waiting at check-in or adjusting settings after you arrive. Everything is ready for you when you get there — just like home, but better.
